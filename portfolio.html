Capstone Research Project

During my Senior Year at UVA, I conducted a Capstone Research on the Socio-technical Implications of “Machine Learning for E-commerce and Transportation.” I learned that tech companies need to streamline their production schedule, improve their apps for autonomous vehicles, and ensure their apps and autonomous vehicles are ready for e-commerce so that customers can wholeheartedly embrace AI and machine learning in their vehicles. Completing capstone research has enabled me to develop critical thinking, research, and problem-solving skills.
